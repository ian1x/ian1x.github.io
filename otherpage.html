About Me

My name is Ian Holland and I am a Graduate Research Assistant at the Space and Atmospheric Instrumentation Lab(SAIL) at Embry Riddle Aeronautical University (ERAU), SAIL page. My primary research is in plasma physics. I work on largely in a theoretical capacity on instrument development, instrument calibration, magnetic field solutions, data analysis and plasma interactions.

I also contribute to work at ERAU's Wave Lab, Wave Lab page, where I am working on a project to charecterize a fluid dynamics phenomena relating to the Leidenfrost effect, Leidenfrost effect Wiki.
